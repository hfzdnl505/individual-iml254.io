About Me

My full name is Hafiz Danial Bin Hamizi. I am a student at MARA University of Technology (UiTM) Kedah branch. I am currently on my fourth semester in pursuing my study for the programme IM144 or Library Informatics.

It is true when they said that time flies really fast. Without realizing it, I have been studying here for almost two years and about to reach the end of my diploma's life.

Let's get to know a little more about me, shall we?

The moment I was born:

I was born in the year of 2005 which means that I am 20 years old according to the year. My birth date is 22 in the month of June. I was born in Kelantan which is one of the states located in Malaysia. The hospital I was born in is commonly known as Kota Bharu General Hospital but its real name is Hospital Raja Perempuan Zainab II.
